Beef Fried Rice

Need to use up day old rice? This simple recipe is just what you need to use it up and it's delicious to boot. A little bit of beef an onion an egg or two some spices and a veggie of your choice and you will quickly have a meal that will fill your belly for not a lot of dough

  • Day old cooked rice(must be cold and dry)
  • One or two beaten eggs
  • One onion(I prefer yellow) finely diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic smashed and chopped
  • 3 tblspn of soy sauce
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • sugar to taste(or you can use hoisin sauce
  • 1/2 lb beef strips or chunks(bite sized)
  • peanut oil(or your preferred frying oil)
  • red chili paste or srirachia
  • 1 or 2 veggies of your choice and amount
  • Optionally you can include ginger and/or spring onion sliced thin

Steps

Make sure to have all of your ingredients prepared before begining to cook. This dish goes together quickly because of the high heat used.

  1. Heat a well oiled wok on very high heat.
  2. Using a few tblspn of oil toss in your beef and brown.
  3. Remove beef once browned and put to the side
  4. Add a bit more oil and toss in the garlic.
  5. Once the garlic is fragrant you can add in your onion.
  6. Add grated ginger at this point if you are using it.
  7. Add in your day old rice and begin the process of breaking it up.
  8. Add your beef back in, and continue breaking up your rice
  9. Add soy sugar and chili paste and mix well.
  10. Make a well at the center of the wok. Add a bit of oil. Toss in your egg and fry till at desired consistency.
  11. Mix/toss frantically.
  12. Toss in your veggies and continue tossing.
  13. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  14. Remove from heat and serve(add spring onion as a topping garnish if desired)
  15. Clean up the massive mess you made frantically moving like a madman/woman(only if you are as messy as I am)
